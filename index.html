About

Hi! I'm Marla. I'm 30, and have thus far have had a wide variety of experiences in my life. I love to travel and explore other cultures, hike, practice yoga, milk goats, and garden. Just recently, I spent the last two years in India studying Ashtanga Yoga and Tibetan Buddhism, and dabbling in learning basic Tibetan language. Here I was able to volunteer with a drug rehab center for refugees. I was inspired by seeing some of the work my friends did helping NGOs in the area with making websites to help locals. I would like to be able to make really awesome websites for other people one day, and help people put their visions into reality on the internet.

Experience

The Farm

Years ago, I purchased a small property in rural Oregon. Here I learned a lot about building, fencing, and such as I sustained myself on the food I was growing. I raised some dairy goats, chickens, pigs, and lots of vegetables. This experience taught me a lot about self-reliance and problem solving.

India

I went to India to go to my teacher's teacher in Mysore to study Ashtanga yoga. Luckily I wound up in a meditation retreat in the Himalayas. Here I decided to continue studies and live. I spent much of the first year in meditation retreats. Later I ended up studying Tibetan language and teaching yoga for a drug rehab for Tibetan refugees. I was even lucky enough to meet the Dalai Lama here. This experience taught me a lot about myself and how my mind works. I think I developed a bit more patience, and hopefully I am a kinder person today as I continue to practice what I began learning here.
